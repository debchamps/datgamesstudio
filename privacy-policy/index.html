Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 27, 2026

This policy applies to DAT Games Studio mobile games and this website. It explains what information may be collected, how it is used, and which third-party services may process information when you use the games.

Information We May Collect

DAT Games Studio games may collect or receive limited information needed to operate, improve, secure, and monetize the games. This can include:

  • Device and app information, such as device model, operating system version, language, app version, and crash diagnostics.
  • Usage information, such as game events, session activity, achievements, preferences, and performance signals.
  • Advertising identifiers or similar identifiers used for ad delivery, measurement, fraud prevention, and frequency controls.
  • Optional information you choose to provide, such as feedback submitted through store reviews, social pages, or direct contact.

How Information Is Used

Information is used to run the games, save preferences, improve gameplay, analyze crashes, understand player engagement, show ads where applicable, prevent abuse, and respond to feedback.

Third-Party Services

The games may use third-party services that collect information according to their own policies. These services can include:

  • Google Play Services
  • Google AdMob
  • Firebase Analytics
  • Meta / Facebook services
  • AppLovin
  • Tappx

Availability of these services can vary by game, platform, country, and app version.

Cookies And Website Data

This website is hosted as a static GitHub Pages site. It does not run its own account system. External links, app stores, social pages, and embedded third-party services may use cookies or similar technologies under their own policies.

Children's Privacy

DAT Games Studio does not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, contact us through the available store or social channels so the information can be reviewed and removed where appropriate.

Security

Reasonable measures are used to protect information processed through the games and services. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, so absolute security cannot be guaranteed.

External Links

The games and website may link to app stores, social networks, support pages, ads, or other third-party websites. DAT Games Studio is not responsible for the content, privacy policies, or practices of those third-party services.

Changes To This Policy

This policy may be updated from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a new effective date.

Contact

For privacy questions, use the contact options available through the relevant app store listing or the DAT Games Facebook page.
